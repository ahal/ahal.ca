Life and Times

My name is Andrew Halberstadt, I'm a 20-something guy from Ontario who works at Mozilla in Toronto. I graduated from the University of Waterloo for Software Engineering. I've been described as stereotypically Canadian and am a man of many trades but master of none.

I enjoy hacking around in Python and Javascript and I have several projects on the go. Sometimes I don't really know why I do them. I suspect I'm a bit of a masochist.

Outside of coding I like sports, hockey (duh) and soccer being my favourites. I really enjoy reading a good book, listening to music and I can (sort of) play guitar and piano. I also dabble with scuba diving, and somewhat more than dabble with beer.
